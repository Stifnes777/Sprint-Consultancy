What is Design Thinking

  • Design Thinking refers to the thinking skills used by practitioners to create novel ideas and solve big problems using an evidence-based approach.
  • Design Thinking offers thinking skills and practices that can support entrepreneurs and professionals in creatively addressing practical problems and challenges. Design Thinkers achieve this by deepening their capacity to address practical problems and challenges.
